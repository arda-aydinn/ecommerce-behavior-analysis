SYNTHETIC DATASET NOTICE
This file describes a synthetic (artificially generated) dataset created for learning, prototyping, and analysis.
It is NOT real customer data and does not correspond to any identifiable individuals.

DATA STORY
The dataset represents order-level records from a Türkiye-based e-commerce platform between 2018 and 2024.
Each row corresponds to a single shopping transaction (order) and contains customer demographics, shopping context,
and order outcomes such as delivery time and satisfaction. The platform sells multiple product categories and supports
common payment methods and acquisition channels.

DATASET SHAPE
- Rows (observations): 1725
- Columns (variables): 16

COLUMN INFORMATION (16 VARIABLES)
1) year (integer): Calendar year of the order. Range: 2018–2024.
2) order_id (string): Unique order identifier (synthetic).
3) gender (categorical): Customer gender. Values: Female, Male.
4) age (integer): Customer age in years. Range: 18–70.
5) city (categorical): Customer city in Türkiye (synthetic assignment). Example values include İstanbul, Ankara, İzmir, etc.
6) membership_tier (categorical): Loyalty/membership level. Values: Guest, Bronze, Silver, Gold.
7) device_type (categorical): Device used during purchase. Values: Mobile, Desktop, Tablet.
8) traffic_source (categorical): Main acquisition channel for the session. Values: Organic, Paid Search, Social, Email, Referral.
9) payment_method (categorical): Payment type. Values: Credit Card, Debit Card, Cash on Delivery, Bank Transfer, Digital Wallet.
10) product_category (categorical): Primary product category in the basket. Values: Electronics, Fashion, Home & Kitchen, Beauty, Sports, Books & Stationery, Groceries.
11) items_in_cart (integer): Number of items purchased in the order. Range: 1–12.
12) basket_value_try (numeric): Final basket value in Turkish Lira (TRY) after discounts.
13) discount_rate (numeric): Discount rate applied to the order (0–0.45).
14) delivery_days (integer): Delivery duration in days. Range: 1–10.
15) satisfaction_score (numeric): Post-delivery customer satisfaction score on a 1.0–5.0 scale (one decimal).
16) repeat_purchase_90d (binary target): Target variable indicating whether the customer made another purchase within 90 days.
    Values: 1 = Yes, 0 = No.

MEASUREMENT SCALES
- Nominal (categorical): gender, city, membership_tier, device_type, traffic_source, payment_method, product_category
- Ratio (numeric with true zero): basket_value_try, items_in_cart, delivery_days, age
- Proportion: discount_rate
- Ordinal/Rating scale: satisfaction_score
- Binary: repeat_purchase_90d

TARGET VARIABLE
- repeat_purchase_90d is recommended for classification tasks (customer retention / repeat purchase prediction).
